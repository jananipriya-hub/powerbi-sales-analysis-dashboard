# UCI Sales Dashboard - Power BI

This project analyzes the UCI Sales dataset using Power BI.

## Features
- Interactive slicers
- Sales trend analysis
- Region-wise performance
- Product category insights
- KPI cards for revenue and sales

## Tools Used
- Power BI
- Excel
- Data Cleaning
- Data Modeling
  
## Key Insights
Here are some insights derived from the analysis:

- Total sales reached 273K with 173K items sold across 948 customers.
- Sales show seasonal patterns with a significant peak in December.
- The United Kingdom contributes the highest share of total sales.
- "Paper Craft Little Birdie" is the top-selling product.
- A small number of products generate most of the revenue, indicating high product concentration.

## Business Recommendations
Here are some recommendations derived from the analysis:

- Increase marketing and inventory during the holiday season to capitalize on peak December sales.
- Expand sales strategies in international markets to reduce heavy dependence on the UK.
- Promote top-performing products like "Paper Craft Little Birdie" through targeted campaigns and bundles.
- Introduce promotions or discounts during low-sales months to stabilize revenue.
- Analyze customer purchasing patterns to improve product assortment and demand forecasting.

## How to Use
Download the .pbix file and open in Power BI Desktop to interact with slicers and visuals.
